Fitness Exercises

YOGA

We offer yoga classes to help reduce stress, lengthen, strengthen, and balance your body and mind. Mats, blocks, and straps are available, and all classes are open to all levels. We also offer fitness classes to improve your core strength and cardio, and complement your climbing.

Fitness Equipments

Our fitness area includes treadmills, bikes, ellipticals, and rowing machines. Free weights (including power-lifting equipment), a cable cross machine, a Marpro rope trainer, and a variety of equipment for body-weight training allow you to hone your strength and work on muscle imbalances.
